MITS Leadership Awards

In the era of exponentially advancing technologies, digital factories are transforming into smart manufacturing organizations, companies are using the evolving technology across value chains and creating growth opportunities.

Master Innovate Transform Sustain - MITS Leadership awards have an objective to acknowledge the transformative leaders who have changed the landscape of manufacturing technology with their revolutionary ideas by constantly innovating and implementing technology in their organizations.

Features
  • An esteemed Jury panel
  • Exposure to over a 100 CIOs, CTOs, CDOs in nomination list
  • Network and interact with short-listed CIOs, CTOs, CDOs

An Award ceremony that brings together approximately 135+ of India’s senior business and Information Technology leaders.

Entry Requirement

  • Holds the title of Chief Information officer or equivalent senior IT leadership position
  • A member of top management
  • The applicant cannot be self-employed
  • The Application form needs to be signed by the Nominee and stamped by the organization.
  • Applicants must present accomplishments related to the applicant’s current position.
  • The final eligibility of the Participant will be subject to the discretion and approval of Jury.
  • Finalists will be expected to attend the Symposium in person.
  • Participants can upload up to three supporting documents (screenshots, photographs, references, project plan, demos etc.) -Max. Attachment size can be 15MB.

Evaluation Criteria for MITS Leader

  • “Vision and Leadership “The CIO must possess sound business acumen, astute foresight and the ability to lead, inspire and guide his/her organization to greater heights of growth, IT achievement and recognition.
  • Knowledge and Experience The CIO must have a sound understanding of all matters relating to the corporate regulatory compliance and reporting requirements of the company.
  • Track Record and Accomplishments The CIO’s track record and accomplishments will be a key factor.

Evaluation Criteria for Indian IT Veteran

  • 30+ years of robust experience in IT domain.
  • Served as CIO for at least 10+ years.
  • Sustained excellence and responsible for bringing the radical IT transformation in an organization.
  • Strong track record of extraordinary Leadership and accountable in decision making of multiple departments.
  • Exceptional track record achievement, the judges will consider the corporate deals, including M&As, rights issues etc., handled by the CIO during the year under review. The degree of difficulty of each corporate deal will also be taken into account. The ability of the CIO to create long-term shareholder value for the company is an important consideration.

Judging Process

The Jury Panel would evaluate based on the information provided in the Nominee Form and the supporting documents shared across by the applicants

  • The size and scope of responsibilities
  • Leadership and management effectiveness
  • Business value created by technology innovation
  • Engagement in industry and community endeavors.

Additional Information

  • Entries submitted without all requirements will not be considered
  • By entering the MITS Leadership Awards, entrants grant permission to reproduce the materials and information entered online in any magazine, book, promotional material, or online/digital format produced by Exito.
  • Failure to provide an interview or significant discrepancies between the entry and information provided after judging could result in forfeiture of the award.
